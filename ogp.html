Three key ares I would like to focus on in this role:

Empowering data collection and analysis

Data collection in the form of surveys and feedback forms are often outsourced to external vendors. How can we utilize existing tools like FormSG to equip public service officers with not only the tools, but the confidence to collect and analyse data?

Creating a single source of truth

Agencies that operate on the ground tend to rely on disparate excel files for data collection, making reconciliation an unnecessarily difficult and painful task. How might we, with the use of experience management systems, create a single source of truth?

Facilitating intra-government data sharing

How can we strike that fine balance between ensuring compliance with data privacy regulations, while also facilitating the free-sharing of data between agencies to enable data-driven policy decisions? More importantly, how can we communicate this to agencies so that they would have the assurance to enable data sharing?
