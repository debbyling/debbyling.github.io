Network Analysis on K-pop Celebrities

A non-exhaustive list of K-pop celebrities and their interactions on Twitter


Summary:

Understanding the network of a selected list of K-pop celebrities by examing how they interact on Twitter. Each node represents a K-pop celebrity, with the node color corresponding to the talent management company that they are signed under.

Features:
  • Hover over each node to see the Artist's name, group (if applicable), and company
  • Drag and drop / zoom in and zoom out of each node around to better navigate around
  • Click on each celebrity to see their immediate connections
  • Search for a K-pop Artist by entering the artist's Twitter Handle in the search bar below
